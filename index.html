Dress Up

for Wonderla

To ensure that the water used in our various water rides is clean and hygienic, we enforce a strict dress code.

  • Always wear apparel made of Nylon or 100% synthetic material (t-shirts, shorts, bermudas, three-fourths, or swim suits)
  • Sarees, churidars/salwars, dupatta, formal pants, shirts, burkhas/ purdahs, school uniforms, denims (jeans and three-fourth jeans), cargos etc. are not permitted in the water rides. Almost all our rides involve motion/moving parts. That’s why we request our guests to refrain from wearing long or loose garments like Saree/Dupatta/Shawls/Scarves/ Hijab/Mafta/Habit etc. in any of the rides, as it could lead to potential harm to themselves or others.
  • In case you are not wearing or carrying garments that fit the bill, You could purchase Nylon/Synthetic clothes (A pair of T-shirt & Shorts start at INR 150.) from the stores in the park near the changing room/locker area at a nominal price.
